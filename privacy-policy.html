Chameleon Stealth Master

Last updated: July 15, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how "Chameleon Stealth Master" (the "Game," "we," "us," or "our") handles information when you download and use our application. We built this Game without collecting personal data from our players, and this policy explains exactly what that means.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or process any personal data.

Specifically, the Game:

  • Does not require account creation or login
  • Does not collect your name, email address, or any other personal identifiers
  • Does not use cloud saves or store your progress on any server
  • Does not include advertising or advertising SDKs
  • Does not include in-app purchases or payment processing
  • Does not use analytics, crash reporting, or any third-party tracking tools
  • Does not request access to your camera, microphone, location, contacts, or storage
  • Does not collect device identifiers, advertising IDs, or usage statistics

All game data (such as your progress or settings, if applicable) is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to us or to any third party. Uninstalling the Game removes this data from your device.

Children's Privacy

The Game is not specifically directed at children and does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children, because it does not collect any information at all.

Data Sharing and Third Parties

Since we do not collect any data, we have nothing to share, sell, or disclose to third parties. The Game does not integrate any third-party services, SDKs, or libraries that collect data.

Your Rights (EU / GDPR)

Because the Game does not collect, store, or process any personal data, there is no personal data held about you that could be accessed, corrected, deleted, or transferred under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) or similar laws. If this ever changes (for example, if we add new features that involve data collection), we will update this Privacy Policy in advance and describe your rights at that time in detail.

Permissions

The Game may request only the minimum device permissions required for it to run and function (for example, internet access if used for non-data-collecting purposes such as loading game assets, or storage access strictly for saving local game data). No permission is used to collect or transmit personal information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy in the future, for example if we add new features to the Game (such as ads, cloud saves, or analytics). Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
